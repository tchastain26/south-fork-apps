# Random Picker Notes

Created: 2026.04.23
Status: Done

## What It Does

Random Picker chooses one option from a user-provided list, supports line-separated or comma-separated entries, shows a short shuffle animation, and keeps a history of the last 10 picks.

## How to Use

Enter options into the text area, one per line or separated by commas. Click Pick One to choose a winner. Use Clear History to reset the pick history.
