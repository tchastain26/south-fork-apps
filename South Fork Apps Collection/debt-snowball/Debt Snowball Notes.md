# Debt Snowball Notes

Created: 2026.04.23
Status: Done

## What It Does

Debt Snowball estimates a payoff plan by ordering debts from smallest balance to largest, applying minimum payments, and rolling freed payments into the next debt.

## How to Use

Add debts with balance, minimum payment, and interest rate, set any extra monthly payment, then review payoff order, months to payoff, total interest, and progress bars.
