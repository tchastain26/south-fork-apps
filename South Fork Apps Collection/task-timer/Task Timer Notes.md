# Task Timer Notes

Created: 2026.04.15
Status: Done

## What It Does

A minimalist stopwatch for tracking deep work sessions. Monitor your progress and build a local history of your task durations.

## How to Use

1. Click "Start" to begin tracking.
2. Click "Stop" to pause.
3. Click "Reset" to clear the clock. 
4. Completed sessions are added to the history list.
