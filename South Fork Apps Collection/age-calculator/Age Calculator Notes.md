# Age Calculator Notes

Created: 2026.04.23
Status: Done

## What It Does

Age Calculator turns a birthdate into exact age, total days alive, estimated heartbeats, estimated breaths, and approximate full moons since birth.

## How to Use

Enter a birthdate and the results update instantly. Future dates are rejected so the stats stay meaningful.
