# Bingo Maker Notes

Created: 2026.04.23
Status: Done

## What It Does

Bingo Maker creates randomized 5x5 bingo cards from a custom list of prompts and includes a simple live caller for running a round in the browser.

## How to Use

Paste at least 24 entries, generate one or more cards, then switch to the caller panel to draw items one at a time.
